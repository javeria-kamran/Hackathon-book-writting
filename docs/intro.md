# Welcome to the Forefront of AI and Robotics

Step into the world of **Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics**, where digital intelligence meets the physical world. This course is your gateway to understanding and mastering the technologies that power the next generation of intelligent machines.

From the foundational principles of Robotic Operating System (ROS 2) to the complexities of creating lifelike digital twins and deploying advanced AI models with NVIDIA Isaac, you will gain hands-on experience with the tools and techniques that are shaping the future.

Our curriculum is designed to take you on a journey from the basics of robotic systems to the cutting edge of Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models. You will learn to build, simulate, and deploy intelligent agents that can perceive, understand, and interact with the world around them.

Whether you are a seasoned developer or just starting your journey in AI and robotics, this guide will provide you with the knowledge and skills to become a pioneer in this exciting field.
